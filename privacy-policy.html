Privacy Policy

Pursuant to Art. 13 of Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR)

Welcome to the Villa Ottelio de Carvalho website. Protecting your privacy is essential to us. With this policy, we wish to explain how we collect, manage, and store your personal data in compliance with European Regulation 2016/679 (GDPR).

1. Data Controller

The Data Controller of your personal data is:
Rodolfo de Carvalho
Registered Office: Località Ottelio 1, 33044 Manzano (UD), Italy.
Contact: rdcservizi [at] gmail [dot] com

2. Types of Data Processed

Through the contact form on the Site, we collect exclusively the personal data that you voluntarily choose to provide to submit your request. Specifically:

  • First and Last Name
  • Email Address
  • Any personal data included in the text of the message.

3. Purposes and Legal Basis

Your personal data will be processed exclusively to respond to your requests for information, organize private visits to the Residence, or provide you with the information dossier.

The legal basis legitimizing this processing is your explicit consent, given via the appropriate checkbox when submitting the form (Art. 6, par. 1, let. a of the GDPR).

4. Processing Methods and Retention

The processing is carried out using IT and telematic tools, adopting all necessary security measures to prevent data loss or unauthorized access.

Your data will be retained for the time strictly necessary to fulfill your request. Subsequently, unless there are different legal obligations or a negotiation is established, the data will be securely deleted within 24 months from the last contact.

5. Communication to Third Parties

Your data will not be disseminated or sold to third parties in any way. Only subjects authorized by the Data Controller or external data processors strictly necessary for the technical functioning of the service (for example, the site's hosting provider or the Web3Forms service used to forward emails from the contact form) may access your data.

6. Your Rights

At any time, pursuant to Articles 15-22 of the GDPR, you have the right to request access to your data, rectification, erasure (Right to be forgotten), restriction, or to object to their processing. You can also withdraw your consent at any time.

To exercise your rights, you can send a written communication to the Data Controller's email address indicated in point 1.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Italian Data Protection Authority (www.garanteprivacy.it) if you believe that the processing violates current regulations.
