2. Structural Focus (The "Clinical Record")

A Massive and Healthy Structure

Villa Ottelio de Carvalho presents a construction solidity from another era, maintained with constant care:

The Roof: This is the structural highlight. The roof was completely replaced approximately 10 years ago, respecting the original aesthetic but ensuring perfect seal and protection.

Walls and Comfort: The villa is built with imposing stone and rock walls (40 cm thick) that ensure invaluable natural coolness in summer. In winter, thanks to cast-iron radiators that retain heat for a long time, the rooms warm up, ensuring excellent living comfort.

Windows: Light is the protagonist thanks to over 100 windows. To improve insulation, 96% of the windows have been updated with double glazing and wooden shutters: a fundamental intervention that retains heat and insulates from external noise while keeping the historical style intact.

3. Systems Upgrade and Tax Incentives

The current diesel heating system, although functional, is technologically outdated. This condition represents the greatest tax advantage for the buyer. The need to replace the heating plant allows immediate access to state incentives for energy efficiency and conservative restoration.

Estimated Investment: A preliminary estimate for a high-efficiency hybrid system ranges between €50,000 and €75,000.

Tax Recovery: Up to 65% of the expenditure is recoverable via Ecobonus, which can be combined with any regional calls dedicated to Historical Residences (to be verified at the time of the project).

4. The Privilege of a Cultural Asset

A Certified Work of Art

Villa Ottelio de Carvalho is not just a real estate property, but an Asset of Historical-Artistic Interest recognized by the Ministry of Culture (Superintendency constraint). This official notification is the maximum guarantee of authenticity and value over time: it certifies that owning this residence means guarding an irreplaceable piece of Italian artistic heritage. The property fully belongs to the restricted circle of Italian Historical Residences, a legal status that confers international prestige and protects the value of the investment in the long term.
