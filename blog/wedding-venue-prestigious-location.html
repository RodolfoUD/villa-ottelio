Wedding Venue: The Prestigious Location Market

Villa Ottelio de Carvalho is not only a residence but also a high-potential commercial asset. The high-profile wedding market is constantly looking for authentic, historical, and logistically equipped locations. The villa, with its consecrated chapel, its monumental halls, and its professional kitchen, is perfectly prepared for this sector.

A Turnkey Business Model

The presence of the Barchessa, already equipped with catering spaces, and the large outdoor gardens allows for the organization of events for hundreds of guests without interfering with the privacy of the main residence. For an investor, the transformation into a "Luxury Wedding Venue" represents a solid ROI strategy in a growing market.
